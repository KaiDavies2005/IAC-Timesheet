Timesheet Help

Start with the week and your details Choose the week commencing date, then fill employee, department, initials, manager name, and manager email if you need an email draft.
Enter work rows by day Add one or more rows for each day. Use project numbers, work codes, location, supervisor, on-call, start time, and finish time.
Typing times Type times directly. The app accepts formats like 07:00, 0700, 2:30pm, and 02:30 PM, then cleans them to 24-hour format.
Clock In and Clock Out Clock buttons create a row for the current day and keep the actual clock log in the overview. The timesheet row can still be edited manually.
Totals and overtime Daily and weekly totals update automatically. Friday, Saturday, and Sunday count as overtime, and any configured break minutes are applied when the worked total is over 6 hours.
Auto indirect When enabled, Monday to Thursday rows that cover the normal 07:00 to 16:30 day can have the configured final hours moved into an Indirect row. Earlier entered work stays the priority, and any time beyond the basic day still counts as overtime.
Excel and email Export to Excel fills the original template only with your entered values. Draft email lets you choose Outlook, Mail, Gmail, or your default mail app. Outlook can preserve your normal signature when that setting is enabled.
